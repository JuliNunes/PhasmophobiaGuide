Phasmophobia Complete Guide

GHOSTS

  1. Spirit

    2. Tips:

    When smudged, can't hunt for 3 minutes instead of the normal 1.5 minutes.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    There is no good test for the Spirit, but when your team is at 0% sanity and the ghost is hunting constantly you can smudge its room and count if it will take 3 minutes or more to begin another hunt (you have to smudge the room, not the Ghost when it's hunting).

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Spirit Box

    Ghost Writing

  2. Wraith

    3. Tips:

    Can't leave footsteps. Be sure to only check for feet if you hear footsteps before assuming its a Wraith. It's possible the ghost doesn't move after stepping on salt.

    Can teleport to your location and leave EMF 2 (or EMF 5). If an EMF 2/5 happens and you saw/heard nothing, start thinking Wraith.

    Can teleport to you while using the music box.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    To test if it's a Wraith put salt on its room, wait for the Ghost to step on salt and walk, if there is no footprint it's 90% a Wraith (that 10% is a possibility to be a Mimic mimicking the Wraith at that moment, so be careful if you got Spirit Box, if you got Ghost Orb then it's a Mimic.)

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Spirit Box

    D.O.T.S Projector

  3. Phantom

    4. Tips:

    Will disappear when you take a photo. You can know it's a Phantom for sure if your photo doesn't have any glitch effect to it, but is written "Ghost".

    Will blink or flash much less frequently during a hunt. If the ghost gets invisible for a more seconds than normal it's a Phantom.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    There are two ways to see if it's a Phantom, when it does a ghost event try your best to take a picture of it, if he is not there but you see "ghost" on the album and there is no glitch effect, well... it's a Phantom. Also, if you took its photo during an event and the event didn't stop it is not a Phantom

    The other way to test is by looking at it when it's hunting, if he gets invisible for too long it's a Phantom, at first will be hard to tell, but with some practice you will be able to tell the difference between a Phantom and other ghosts really easy.

    Evidence:

    Spirit Box

    Fingerprints

    D.O.T.S Projector

  4. Poltergeist

    5. Tips:

    Can throw a bunch of things at once.

    Can throw things at longer distance.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Poltergeist test is fun, you have to put many items in the middle of its room, if he throw all, or most, items at the same time, you know it's a Polter (remember, The Mimic, can mess up all tests that I show here).

    Evidence:

    Spirit Box

    Fingerprints

    Ghost Writing

  5. Banshee

    6. Tips:

    Can only kill one person who is the target at a time, that means if you are not the target you can walk directly to him and he will ignore you (good luck finding out if you are or not the target).

    If the target is not in the house he will hunt normally being able to kill anyone he sees.

    Hunts are based on the target's sanity instead of the average sanity like other ghosts.

    More likely to do singing ghost events and will drain the target's sanity more when performing a ghost event on them.

    Has a unique scream that can be heard with the parabolic microphone.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% target player sanity (depends on the player)

    Test:

    Banshee test can be tricky, all living members of your team have to be inside the house you must pick one to be a bait, the bait should be the only one not hiding and is best to be in a place where is not normally where the ghost walk (for example behind the kitchen balcony on Willow Street) or a place where you can loop it, if the ghost completly ignores you, you can be sure it's a Banshee and you are not it's target (remember the Ghost may walk to you, but may not chase you). If it comes to you smudge it and get out. Then you can try again with another bait, the Banshee can only have one target and it doens't change unless the target died (if the target is outside the house the Banshee hunts like any other Ghost, chasing and killing anyone it sees).

    Another test is using the Parabolic Microphone pointing it to the Ghost Room, you have a small chance of hearing the Banshee Scream.

    Evidence:

    Fingerprints

    Ghost Orb

    D.O.T.S Projector

  6. Jinn

    7. Tips:

    Can't turn off the breaker.

    Will be faster while farther away when the breaker is on (if it's off he can't use that abillity).

    Has a random chance to drain 25% sanity with no visual or audio queues when you're close enough to it.

    Ghost Speed:

    2.5 m/s (fast) when far from player with the breaker on

    1.6 when close (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Jinn test is easy, turn on the breaker go to a place where you can loop, then watch it carefully, is the Ghost coming fast and then when it is close to you it slows down? It's a Jinn! If you are not sure try again with the breaker off and check if you still notice a different speed when it is close.

    If the Ghost turns off the breaker it isn't a Jinn.

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Fingerprints

    Freezing Temperatures

  7. Mare

    8. Tips:

    Can't turn on lights.

    More likely to roam from rooms with lights on.

    Can hunt earlier with the lights off and later with lights on.

    More likely to explode lights.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    60% team sanity if light on

    40% team sanity if light off

    Test:

    Turn on the light of its room and see he it is burning crucifixes or hunting inside the room, if she hunts with the light on, it's not a Mare, if she turns the light off and then hunts or start a hunt outside the room where the light is off it's probably a Mare.

    Evidence:

    Spirit Box

    Ghost Orb

    Ghost Writing

  8. Revenant

    9. Tips:

    Extremely fast when it has line of sight of a player, and will be extremely slow while it sees no one.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.1 m/s (very slow) if the ghost is not chasing

    3.0 m/s (very fast) if the ghost is chasing

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Revenant for me is one of the most easy to tell, just wait for it to hunt and stay hidden, listen closely to it's steps, Revenant steps when he sees no one is EXTREMELY slow and easy to distinguish. You can also try to do the test again but this time letting it see you, but I won't recommend that since he will be EXTREMELY fast chasing you, even with a smudge ready you might miss it.

    Evidence:

    Ghost Orb

    Ghost Writing

    Freezing Temperatures

  9. Shade

    10. Tips:

    Can't interact with anything or hunt when someone is in the same room (can start a hunt in a room where there's no one, even if it's not the Ghost Room).

    More likely to do non-physical ghost events.

    Shade has a chance of appearing as a black shadow when walking towards the Music Box.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    35% team sanity

    Test:

    I like to say that, if the ghost is extremely boring, it is probably a Shade. If it isn't hunting and not doing anything you can get your people out of the house and let only one there, if it start interacting more is probably a Shade, also if it start hunts far from it's room when there are people there you can think Shade too. (Remember that Shade only start huntings at 35% sanity)

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Ghost Writing

    Freezing Temperatures

  10. Demon

    11. Tips:

    Can rarely hunt at *any*% sanity.

    When smudged, can't hunt for 1 minute instead of the normal 1.5 minutes.

    Smudges during hunt don't last as long than the other ghosts.

    Are affected by crucifixes from a much larger distance (5m instead the normal 3m).

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    70% team sanity

    Chance of hunting at *any*% sanity

    Test:

    I don't think there's actually a test for Demon, well, if it start hunting when you just got in the house it's a Demon (or a Mimic, of course), also you can check if it eats crucifixes really fast, or is hunting constantly, it can be an indicative of a Demon.

    Other way to know is if it start the hunt at the beggining go check your team sanity, if it is between 70% and 60% it's probably a Demon

    You can smudge it and count if it'll hunt before 1.5 minutes

    Evidence:

    Fingerprints

    Ghost Writing

    Freezing Temperatures

  11. Yurei

    12. Tips:

    Can close a door completely and it will drain of 15% sanity of nearby players.

    Smudging will prevent roaming for 90 seconds (not during a hunt).

    Yurei is more likely to do the airball event.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Yurei can't leave the Ghost Room for 90 seconds after smudging, so use a Motion Sensor to check if it'll leave.

    If you are not in Nighmare Mode you can check your sanity constantly, if it's dropping weirdly fast, you can suspect Yurei.

    You can also pay close attention to doors, if there is a door completely open and the ghost closes it completely you can be sure it's a Yurei (again, it could be a Mimic).

    Evidence:

    Ghost Orb

    Freezing Temperatures

    D.O.T.S Projector

  12. Oni

    13. Tips:

    Cannot do the hiss/"air ball" ghost event.

    Makes players lose sanity faster when doing an Ghost Event.

    Blink faster (is more visible) during a hunt.

    Has very strong throws, if something was thrown very far, you can start thinking Oni did that.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    I don't think there is a test for Oni, but you can suspect it's him if it is doing too much ghost events, specially at the beggining of the round.

    Also, if he does the air ball event it's not an Oni.

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Freezing Temperatures

    D.O.T.S Projector

  13. Yokai

    14. Tips:

    Can hunt as early as 80% sanity if talking near it.

    Can't detect equipment and voice in more than 2 meters range during a hunt.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    80% team sanity if talking near it

    Test:

    You and your team are talking too much in the Ghost Room and the ghost is mad? Might be a Yokai.

    You can also test by calling it when it's hunting, if it is too dumb to find you it might be a Yokai. It's specially easy to test this on Willow Street, you have to stay behind the glass kitchen door and with your flashligh on, if your flahsligh is flashing and the ghost goes to the garage or elsere that is not to you, it's a Yokai.

    Evidence:

    Spirit Box

    Ghost Orb

    D.O.T.S Projector

  14. Hantu

    15. Tips:

    Can't turn on the breaker.

    Twice as likely to turn off the breaker.

    Does not speed up during hunts and can be looped in a warm room.

    Always has freezing as evidence on Nightmare.

    Can show freezing during a hunt.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.4 m/s (little slower) when in warm rooms (outside of the Ghost Room if the breaker is on)

    2.7 m/s (fast) when in cold rooms (in the Ghost Room or when the breaker is off)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Is the ghost fast on its room but slows down when out of it (somewhere hot), it's a Hantu.

    Another test is, let it chase you, if its speed didn't change while chasing, it's a Hantu

    Evidence:

    Fingerprints

    Ghost Orb

    *Freezing Temperatures

  15. Goryo

    16. Tips:

    Cannot perform a long roam, but can still roam.

    Less likely to perform a short roam.

    Doesn't show DOTS if anyone is in the same room as the ghost.

    Goryo's DOTS only show up on video camera.

    Always has DOTS evidence on nightmare.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    No D.O.T.S yet? Get everyone out of its room and check on the camera. Now you see D.O.T.S? It is (probably) a Goryo.

    To be sure you must have someone by the door of the Ghost Room (but outside) and you look at the camera and your friend looks in person if only you saw the D.O.T.S then it's a Goryo, also if someone saw D.O.T.S in person you can take Goryo out of the list of suspects.

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Fingerprints

    *D.O.T.S Projector

  16. Myling

    17. Tips:

    More likely to make noises on the Parabolic Microphone.

    The only noise quieter than other ghosts during a hunt is the footsteps.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Listen closely to the ghost footsteps, if it really low it is a Myling.

    Another way to tell is by dropping a flashligh on by your hiding spot and pay attention if you can only hear the footsteps when the flashligh is flashing. With other ghosts you will be able to hear the footsteps first.

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Fingerprints

    Ghost Writing

  17. Onryo

    18. Tips:

    Can hunt as early as 60% sanity unless there is a candel nearby.

    More likely to blow out candles.

    After 3 candles have been blown out, each consecutive blown out candle has a 50% chance to start a hunt regardeless of sanity.

    Each dead player increases the chance to hunt after blowing out candles by 25%, that means if 2 players are dead the chance is 100% of hunt after a blown out candle.

    Candles work similarly to crucifixes, it will be blown if onryo tries to hunt and block his hunt (candles take priority than crucifixes).

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Put candles on the Ghost Room, if the ghost start a hunt inside the room with the candles on it's definitely not an Onryo. Be careful, if you keep lighting the candles it may start a hunt outside the room far from the candle range.

    Evidence:

    Spirit Box

    Ghost Orb

    Freezing Temperatures

  18. The Twins

    19. Tips:

    One ghost, but has a decoy that can interact on other rooms far away.

    The main twin will have 90% ghost speed while the decoy twin will have 110% ghost speed.

    Crucifixes only need to be placed where the main twin is.

    Will often leave "slope changing" lines on the activity chart. If the lines changes slopes without leveling off it's almost always the twins.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.4 m/s (little slower) when it's the Ghost huntint

    1.7 (little faster) when it's the Decoy hunting

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Pay attention to the ghost speed every hunt, if you notice that sometimes it's a little bit slower than normal and sometimes is a little bit faster than normal, it's a twins.

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Spirit Box

    Freezing Temperatures

  19. Raiju

    20. Tips:

    Can hunt at 65% sanity when there is active eletronic equipment nearby.

    Also much faster when active eletronic equipment is nearby.

    Causing glitching of all eletronic equipment in a larger range.

    Building's lights and the breaker do not affect the Raiju.

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal) when no turned on equipment is close to it

    2.5 m/s (fast) when turned on equipment is close to it

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity when no turned on equipment close

    65% team sanity when turned on equipment is close

    Test:

    Drop some equipment close to your hiding spot, if when the equipment starts beeping at the hunt and the ghost gets faster you can know it's a Raiju. Just be careful if the ghost is already fast because there is some equipment where it is.

    A better and safer way to test is, let it hunt with all equipments on (careful to not die, since Raiju is extremely fast when there is equipment on), then, turn all the equipments off and check if the ghost speed decreased on the next hunt.

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    Ghost Orb

    D.O.T.S Projector

  20. Obake

    21. Tips:

    25% chance to not leave fingerprints on anything.

    1/6 chance to leave a special fingerprint with an extra finger.

    Can use an ability to reduce the timer for all fingerprints, causing them to disappear more quickly.

    Always has fingerprints on Nightmare

    Ghost Speed:

    1.6 m/s (normal)

    Hunts possible at:

    50% team sanity

    Test:

    Check every fingerprint, if there is a hand with 6 fingers, it's an Obake (remember, can always be a Mimic).

    Also, fingerprints are 100% chance of showing except for Obake, which is 75% so if there's no fingerprint after touching something and then it appears later, it is a Obake. Be sure to check for fingerprint the moment it touches something since Obake has an ability to eraese fingerprints fast.

    Evidence:

    EMF 5

    *Fingerprints

    Ghost Orb

  21. The Mimic

    22. Tips:

    Can mimic any ghost, including The Mimic and a player (if he do that he will be a normal ghost with no special traits).

    Can perform any special ability of any ghost it mimics.

    Will change the mimicking ghost type between every 30 seconds to 2 minutes, but never during a hunt.

    You can see orbs when the ghost is a mimic even though it doesn't have that as evidence.

    Always has "fake" orbs on Nightmare.

    Ghost Speed:

    Depends on what Ghost it is mimicking

    Hunts possible at:

    Depends on what Ghost it is mimicking

    Test:

    If the ghost is changing behaviour too much (is extremely fast one hunt, then slow, then fast again) you can suspect Mimic. Also, if it has, for example, Freezing Temperatures and a 6 finger fingerprint, it's a Mimic that is mimicking the Obake.

    If there is 4 evidences (or 3 in Nighmare Mode) it's a Mimic.

    Evidence:

    Spirit Box

    Fingerprints

    Freezing Temperatures

    *FAKE Ghost Orb

  22. Moroi

    23. Tips:

    Curses players who use and get an answer from the Sipirt Box or Parabolic Microphone. This is cured by taking sanity pills.

    Passive sanity drain is doubled when cursed and will still drain when on rooms with lights on.

    Faster when the average sanity is lower.

    Smudging during a hunt will repel the Moroi for twice as long (12s instead of the normal 6s).

    Always has Spirit Box as evidence on Nightmare.

    Ghost Speed:

    Starts at 1.4 m/s (little slow)

    Gradually reach 2.1 m/s (kinda fast) the lower the team sanity is

    Hunts possible at:

    45% team sanity

    Test:

    First test is, did he responded someone on Spirit Box? Check it's sanity, if it lows down fast it's an Moroi.

    Second test is, when it first hunts, check the ghost speed. Than get your team sanity lower and let it hunt again, check if it got faster, if it did, it's an Moroi. If you didn't pay attention to the speed when your team had sanity, just take some pills and let it hunt, then check if it became slower.

    Evidence:

    *Spirit Box

    Ghost Writing

    Freezing Temperatures

  23. Deogen

    24. Tips:

    Can hunt only at 40% sanity.

    Will be very fast when far away, but will be the slowest ghost when close.

    You can't hide from him, you have to run or just walk, it's easy because he is extremely slow when close.

    Has a 33% chance to perform a special Spirit Box sound when asked any question and when you are very close (like in his exact same position it sounds like a heavy breathing.

    Blinks very quickly during hunts, it means is very visible.

    Always has Spirit Box as evidence on Nightmare.

    Ghost Speed:

    0.4 m/s (slowest ghost) when far to player

    3 m/s (fastest ghost) when close to player

    Hunts possible at:

    40% team sanity

    Test:

    Did the ghost went directly and killed your friend that was hiding? Did he yelled "It's a fucking Deogen"? well, it's a Deogen.

    Deogen test is, don't hide, stay in an open area where you have space to loop it, let it come to you, I know, it will be fast and scary, but have faith it will stop, if the ghost did slow extremely down, just keep going backwards or looping and it will never catch you, then you will be sure it's a Deogen. If it doesn't slow down, smudge the hell out.

    Another test is, put some salt in the room, the Ghost will walk on it, follow the Footprint, when it stops you use a Spirit Box. You might hear the special Deogen Spirit Box sound.

    Evidence:

    *Spirit Box

    Ghost Writing

    D.O.T.S Projector

  24. Thaye

    25. Tips:

    Thaye is the Aging Ghost, it will begin as an extremelly OP Ghost, but will gradually become a weak Ghost because it will age.

    Has a chance to age every 1 to 2 minutes and every 30 seconds if the previous aging attempt failed. Aging succeeds if a player is near the ghost when it attempts to age.

    Can age up to 10 times.

    When asking thw Ouija Board the Thaye's age, the responde will increase as the Thaye ages.

    Ghost Speed:

    Start at 2.7 m/s (fast)

    Gradually reach 1.0 (slow) as the match goes

    Hunts possible at:

    Starts at 75% team sanity

    Gradually reach at 35% team sanity as the match goes

    Test:

    Always pay attention to ghost speed at the very first hunt, if it's really fast, but it is slowing down after some hunts, it's a Thaye. (It will only slow down by time and if there is someone on it's room, if you and your team are usually outside it will age slower, therefore it will be slower to get to a slower ghost speed).

    Extra Info

    Initial interaction / Ghost event rate is 200%, -15% as it ages, final condition is 50% interaction rate

    -6% hunt sanity need to begin a hunt as it ages, final condition is 35%

    -0.175 m/s speed as it ages, final condition is 1.0 m/s

    Evidence:

    Ghost Orb

    Ghost Writing

    D.O.T.S Projector
