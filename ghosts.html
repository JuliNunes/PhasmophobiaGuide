  1. Spirit

      2. Tips

    • When Smudged, can't hunt for 3 minutes instead of the normal 1.5 minutes.

      • Test

      There is no good test for the Spirit, but when your team is at 0% sanity and the ghost is hunting constantly you you can smudge it and count if it will take 3 minutes or more to begin another hunt.

      EMF 5

      Spirit Box

      Ghost Writing

  2. Wraith

      3. Tips

    • Can't leave footsteps. Be sure to only check for feet if you hear footsteps before assuming it's a wraith. It's possible the ghost doesn't move after stepping in salt.
    • Can teleport to your location and leave and EMF 2 (or EMF 5). If an EMF 2/5 happens and you saw/heard nothing, start thinking Wraith.
    • Can teleport to you while using the music box.

      • Test

      The best way to test if it's a Wraith is by putting salt on it's room and waiting for it to step on it and walk, if there is no footprint it's 90% a Wraith (that 10% is a possibility to be a Mimic mimicking the Wraith at that moment, so be careful if there is a suspition of Mimic.)

      Evidence:

      EMF 5

      Spirit Box

      D.O.T.S Projector

  3. Phantom

      4. Tips

    • Will disappear when you take a photo. You can know it's a phantom for sure if your photo doesn't have any glitch effect to it, but it still says "Ghost".
    • Will blink or flash much less frequently during a hunt. If the ghost gets invisible for a more seconds than normal it's a phantom.

      • Test

      There are two ways to see if it's a Phantom, when it does a ghost event try your best to take a picture of it, if he is not there but you see "ghost" on the album and there is no glitch effect, well... it's a Phantom.

      The other way to test is by looking at it when it's hunting, if he gets invisible for too long it's a Phantom, at first will be hard to tell, but with some practice you will be able to tell the difference between a Phantom and other ghosts really easy.

      Evidence:

      Spirit Box

      Fingerprints

      D.O.T.S Projector

  4. Poltergeist

      5. Tips

    • Can throw a bunch of things at once. A good way to test is by making a pile of objects in his room and see if he will throw them all (or most) at the same exact time.

      • Test

      Poltergeist test is fun, you have to put many items in the middle of his room, if he throw all, or most, items at the same time, you know it's a Polter (remember, The Mimic, can mess up all tests that I show here).

      Evidence:

      Spirit Box

      Fingerprints

      Ghost Writing

  5. Banshee

      6. Tips

    • Can only kill one person who is the target at a time, that means if you are not the target you can walk directly to him and he will ignore you (good luck finding out if you are or not the target).
    • If the target is not in the house he will hunt normally being able to kill anyone he sees.
    • Hunts are based on the target's sanity instead of the average sanity like other ghosts.
    • More likely to do singing ghost events and will drain the target's sanity more when performing a ghost event on them.
    • Has a unique scream that can be heard with the parabolic microphone.

      • Test

      Banshee test can be tricky, all live members of your team have to be inside the house you must pick one to be a bait, the bait should be the only one not hiding and is best to be in a place where is not normally where the ghost walk (for example behind the kitchen balcony on Willow Street) if the ghost completly ignores you, you can be sure it's a Banshee and you are not it's target. If it comes to you I'll hope you have a smudge stick ready. Then you can try again with another bait, the Banshee can only have one target and it doens't change unless the target is not inside the house or it died.

      Evidence:

      Fingerprints

      Ghost Orb

      D.O.T.S Projector

  6. Jinn

      7. Tips

    • Can't turn off the breaker.
    • Will be faster while farther away when the breaker is on (if it's off he can't use that abillity).
    • Has a random chance to drain 25% sanity with no visual or audio queues when close you're close enough to it.

      • Test

      Jinn test is very tricky for me personally, if the generator is on and he is fast when far away from people, you can try to turn the generator off and check if he still has the same speed.

      Evidence:

      EMF 5

      Fingerprints

      Freezing Temperatures

  7. Mare

      8. Tips

    • Can't turn on lights.
    • More likely to roam from rooms with lights on.
    • Can hunt earlier (60%) with the lights off and later (40%) with lights on.
    • More likely to explode lights.

      • Test

      For Mare, you turn on the light of her room and see he she is burning crucifixes or hunting inside the room, if she hunts with the light on, you can be sure it's not a Mare, if she turns the light off and then hunts or start a hunt outside the room where the light is off it's probably a Mare.

      Evidence:

      Spirit Box

      Ghost Orb

      Ghost Writing

  8. Revenant

      9. Tips

    • Extremely fast when it has line of sight of a player, and will be extremely slow while it sees no one.

      • Test

      Revenant for me is one of the most easy to tell, just wait for it to hunt and stay hidden, listen closely to it's steps, Revenant steps when he sees no one is EXTREMELY slow. You can also try to do the test again but this time letting it see you, but I won't recommend that since he will be EXTREMELY fast chasing you, even with a smudge ready you might miss it.

      Evidence:

      Ghost Orb

      Ghost Writing

      Freezing Temperatures

  9. Shade

      10. Tips

    • Can't hunt until 35% sanity.
    • Can't interact with anything or junt when someone is in the same room.
    • More likely to do non-physical ghost events.

      • Test

      I like to say that, if the ghost is extremely boring, it's probably a Shade. If it's not hunting and not doing anything you can get your people out of the house and let only one there, if it start interacting more is probably a Shade, also if it start hunts far from it's room when there are people there you can think Shade too. (Remember that Shade only start huntings at 35% sanity)

      Evidence:

      EMF 5

      Ghost Writing

      Freezing Temperatures

  10. Demon

      11. Tips

    • Can hunt at 70% sanity.
    • Can rarely hunt at *any*% sanity.
    • When smudged, can't hunt for 1 minute instead of the normal 1.5 minutes.
    • Smudges during hunt don't last as long than the other ghosts.
    • Are affected by crucifixes from a much larger distance (5m instead the normal 3m).

      • Test

      I don't think there's actually a test for Demon, well, if it start hunting when you just got in the house it's a Demon (or a Mimic, of course), also you can check if it eats crucifixes really fast, or is hunting constantly can be an indicative of a Demon.

      Other way to know is if it start the hunt at the beggining go check your team sanity, if it is between 70% and 60% it's probably a Demon

      Evidence:

      Fingerprints

      Ghost Writing

      Freezing Temperatures

  11. Yurei

      12. Tips

      You can also pay close attention to doors, if there is a door completely open and the ghost closes it completely you can be sure it's a Yurei (again, it could be a Mimic).

  14. Hantu

      15. Tips

    • Can't turn on the breaker.
    • Twice as likely to turn off the breaker.
    • Does not speed up during hunts and can be looped in a warm room.
    • Always has freezing as evidence on Nightmare.

      • Test

      Is the ghost fast on its room but slows down when out of it (somewhere hot), it's a Hantu.

      Evidence:

      Fingerprints

      Ghost Orb

      Freezing Temperatures

  15. Goryo

      16. Tips

    • Cannot perform a long roam, but can still roam.
    • Less likely to perform a short roam.
    • Doesn't show DOTS if anyone is in the same room as the ghost.
    • Goryo's DOTS only show up on video camera.
    • Always has DOTS evidence on nightmare.

      • Test

      No D.O.T.S yet? Get everyone out of its room and check on the camera. Now you see D.O.T.S? It's (probably) an Goryo.

      To be sure you must have someone by the door of the ghost room (but outside) and you look at the camera and your friend looks in person only you saw the D.O.T.S? Then it's a Goryo, also if someone saw D.O.T.S in person you can take Goryo out of the list of suspects.

      Evidence:

      EMF 5

      Fingerprints

      D.O.T.S Projector

  16. Myling

      17. Tips

    • More likely to make noises on the Parabolic Microphone.
    • The only noise quieter than other ghosts during a hunt is the footsteps.

      • Test

      Listen closely to the ghost footsteps, if it really low you can suspect Myling

      Another way to tell is by dropping a flashligh on by your hiding spot and pay attention if you can only hear the footsteps when the flashligh is flashing. With other ghosts you will be able to hear the footsteps first.

      Evidence:

      EMF 5

      Fingerprints

      Ghost Writing

  17. Onryo

      18. Tips

    • Can hunt as early as 60% sanity unless there is a candel nearby.
    • More likely to blow out candles.
    • After 3 candles have been blown out, each consecutive blown out candle has a 50% chance to start a hunt regardeless of sanity.
    • Each dead player increases the chance to hunt after blowing out candles by 25%, that means if 2 players are dead the chance is 100% of hunt after a blown out candle.
    • Candles work similarly to crucifixes, it will be blown if onryo tries to hunt and block his hunt (candles take priority than crucifixes).

      • Test

      Put candles on the ghost room, if the ghost start a hunt inside the room with the candles on it's definitely not an Onryo. Be careful, if you keep lighting the candles it may start a hunt outside the room far from the candle range.

      Evidence:

      Spirit Box

      Ghost Orb

      Freezing Temperatures

  18. The Twins

      19. Tips

    • One ghost, but has a decoy that can interact on other rooms far away.
    • The main twin will have 90% ghost speed while the decoy twin will have 110% ghost speed.
    • Crucifixes only need to be placed where the main twin is.
    • Will often leave "slope changing" lines on the activity chart. If the lines changes slopes without leveling off it's almost always the twins.

      • Test

      Pay attention to the ghost speed every hunt, if you notice that sometimes it's a little bit slower than normal and sometimes is a little bit faster than normal, it's a twins.

      Evidence:

      EMF 5

      Spirit Box

      Freezing Temperatures

  19. Raiju

      20. Tips

    • Can hunt at 65% sanity when there is active eletronic equipment nearby.
    • Also much faster when active eletronic equipment is nearby.
    • Causing glitching of all eletronic equipment in a larger range.
    • Building's lights and the breaker do not affect the Raiju.

      • Test

      Drop some equipment close to your hiding spot, if when the equipment starts beeping at the hunt and the ghost gets faster you can know it's a Raiju. Just be careful if the ghost is already fast because there is some equipment where it is.

      A better and safer way to test is, let it hunt with all equipments on (careful to not die, since Raiju is extremely fast when there is equipment on), then, turn all the equipments off and check if the ghost speed decreased on the next hunt.

      Evidence:

      EMF 5

      Ghost Orb

      D.O.T.S Projector

  20. Obake

      21. Tips

    • 25% chance to not leave fingerprints on anything.
    • 1/6 chance to leave a special fingerprint with an extra finger.
    • Can use an ability to reduce the timer for all fingerprints, causing them to disappear more quickly.
    • Always has fingerprints on Nightmare

      • Test

      There's no test for Obake that I know, only check every fingerprint, if there is a hand with 6 fingers, it's an Obake (remember, can always be a Mimic).

      Evidence:

      EMF 5

      Fingerprints

      Ghost Orb

  21. The Mimic

      22. Tips

    • Can mimic any ghost, including The Mimic and a player (if he do that he will be a normal ghost with no special traits).
    • Can perform any special ability of any ghost it mimics.
    • Will change the mimicking ghost type between every 30 seconds to 2 minutes, but never during a hunt.
    • You can see orbs when the ghost is a mimic even though he doesn't have that as evidence.
    • Always has "fake" orbs on Nightmare.

      • Test

      If the ghost is changing behaviour too much (is extremely fast one hunt, then slow, then fast again) you can suspect Mimic. Also, if it has, for example, Freezing Temperatures and a 6 finger fingerprint, it's a Mimic that is mimicking the Obake.

      If there is 4 evidences (or 3 in Nighmare Mode) it's a Mimic.

      Evidence:

      Spirit Box

      Fingerprints

      Freezing Temperatures

  22. Moroi

      23. Tips

    • Curses players who use and get an answer from the Sipirt Box or Parabolic Microphone. This is cured by taking sanity pills.
    • Passive sanity drain is doubled when cursed and will still drain when on rooms with lights on.
    • Faster when the average sanity is lower.
    • Smudging during a hunt will repel the Moroi for twice as long (12s instead of the normal 6s).
    • Always has Spirit Box as evidence on Nightmare.

      • Test

      First test is, did he responded someone on Spirit Box? Check it's sanity, if it lows down fast it's an Moroi.

      Second test is, when it first hunts, check the ghost speed. Than get your team sanity lower and let it hunt again, check if it got faster, if it did, it's an Moroi. If you didn't pay attention to the speed when your team had sanity, just take some pills and let it hunt, then check if it became slower.

      Evidence:

      Spirit Box

      Ghost Writing

      Freezing Temperatures

  23. Deogen

      24. Tips

    • Can hunt only at 40% sanity.
    • Will be very fast when far away, but will be the slowest ghost when close.
    • You can't hide from him, you have to run or just walk, it's easy because he is extremely slow when close.
    • Has a 33% chance to perform a special Spirit Box sound when asked any question and when you are very close (like in his exact same position it sounds like a heavy breathing.
    • Blinks very quickly during hunts, it means is very visible.
    • Always has Spirit Box as evidence on Nightmare.

      • Test

      Did the ghost went directly and killed your friend that was hiding? Did he yelled "It's a fucking Deogen"? well, it's a Deogen.

      Deogen test is, don't hide, stay in an open area where you have space to run away from it, let it come to you, I know, it will be fast and scary, but have faith it will stop, if the ghost did slow extremely down, just keep going backward and it will never catch you, then you will be sure it's a Deogen. If it doesn't slow down, smudge the hell out.

      Evidence:

      Spirit Box

      Ghost Writing

      D.O.T.S Projector

  24. Thaye

      25. Tips

    • Has a chance to age every 1 to 2 minutes and every 30 seconds if the previous aging attempt failed. Aging succeeds if a player is near the ghost when it attempts to age.
    • Can age up to 10 times.
    • When asking thw Ouija Board the Thaye's age, the responde will increase as the Thaye ages.

      • Test

      Always pay attention to ghost speed at the very first hunt, if it's really fast, but it is slowing down after some hunts, it's a Thaye. (It will only slow down by time and if there is someone on it's room, if you and your team are usually outside it will age slower, therefore it will be slower to get to a slower ghost speed).

      Evidence:

      Ghost Orb

      Ghost Writing

      D.O.T.S Projector
