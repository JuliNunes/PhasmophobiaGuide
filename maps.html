"Possible Hiding Spot" means that is not a hiding spot, but it will appear every time (it doesn's change) and is more or less 90% chance to work hiding there.

Tanglewood Street House Guide

The guide is mostly the map on the left with all locations, so here I will just put few more information.

Tanglewood is a small map (Tanglewood and Willow are the smallest maps), it has only 2 floors (first floor and basemant) and is well known beucase it is the tutorial map of the game.

The possible hiding spot in this map is behind a chiffonier in the dinning room, you can crounch there and it's very likely that you will stay alive if the ghost haven't seen you or sensed you (so turn off your equipment before you hide there).

Some say it is a good map for beginners, but I disagree, because it has one big problem, the ghost room can be in one of the hallways and this may get new player easily confused, the ghost will interact to a lot of places since the hallway is really big.
