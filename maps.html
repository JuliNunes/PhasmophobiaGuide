Maps

"Possible Hiding Spot" means that is an unchangeable spot (every game it will be there), while Hiding Spots may be blocked and is more or less 90% chance that the Ghost won't go there.

Tanglewood Street House Guide

The guide is mostly the map on the left with all locations, so here I will just put few more information.

Tanglewood is a small map (Tanglewood and Willow are the smallest maps), it has only 2 floors (first floor and basement) and is well known because it is the tutorial map of the game.

The Possible Hiding Spot in this map is behind a chiffonier in the dinning room, you can crounch there and it's very likely that you will stay alive if the ghost haven't seen you or sensed you (so turn off your equipment before you hide there).

I also don't advise hiding in the Foyer Hiding Spot on the right (close to the Living Room), the Ghost usually opens a lot that door. My favourites Hiding Spots in this map is the one in the Basement and Nursery and the Possible Hiding Spot in the Dining Room because there's no door that the Ghost can open.

Some say it is a good map for beginners, but I disagree, because if the Ghost Room is in the Foyer or Living Room it may get new player easily confused, the ghost will interact to a lot of places since those hallways are really big (if the Ghost is in the Living Room it will interact with the Hallway in front of it, where it has the door to the Basement and Utility Room).
