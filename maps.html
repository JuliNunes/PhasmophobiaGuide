Tanglewood Street House Guide

The guide is mostly the map on the left with all locations, so here I will just put few more information.

Tanglewood is a small map (Tanglewood and Willow are the smallest maps), it has only 2 floors (first floor and basement) and is well known because it is the tutorial map of the game.

Possible Hiding Spot info

The Possible Hiding Spot on this map is behind a chiffonier on the dinning room, you can crounch there and it's very likely that you will stay alive if the ghost haven't seen you or sensed you (so turn off your equipment before you hide there).

Best and Worst place for Ghost Room

The worst Ghost Room you may get on this map are on the Foyer and on the Living Room because those are big and can be easily confused with other places, also Utility Room is no good either, since it means the Ghost will interact other places, like Garage and Kitchen, because it has almost nothing inside the Utility Room. The best is probably Nursery, because is small, but not that small and is easier to get evidence.

More info

I also don't advise hiding inside the Foyer Hiding Spot on the right (close to the Living Room), the Ghost usually opens a lot that door. My favourites Hiding Spots in this map is the one in the Basement and Nursery and the Possible Hiding Spot in the Dining Room because there's no door that the Ghost can open.

Some say it is a good map for beginners, but I disagree, because if the Ghost Room is in the Foyer or Living Room it may get new player easily confused, the ghost will interact to a lot of places since those hallways are really big (if the Ghost is in the Living Room it will interact with the Hallway in front of it, where it has the door to the Basement and Utility Room).

Willow Street House Guide

Willow is the second smallest map this game has, it is one of my favourite maps, it has only first floor and basement like Tanglewood.

Possible Hiding Spot info

The Possible Hiding Spots on this map are, behind the washing machine in the Utility Room, behind the tool desk in the Garage (I died on this spot a few times, but I still usually hide there) and behind the desk inside the Master Bedroom.

Best and Worst place for Ghost Room

The worst place for Ghost Room you may get is the Basement Hallway, because it's really big, Garage is no good either. The best is probably the Bathroom or kitchen, they are small, easy to get evidence, easy to use crucifixes and there are a lot of stuff for the Ghost to interact with. The rooms in Basement might be good to for the same reason, but it's scarier to stay there.
