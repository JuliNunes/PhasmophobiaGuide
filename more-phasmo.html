Main Equipment

Spirit Box

Spirit Box is really simple, just turn it on and ask questions to the Ghost in the Ghost Room you don't need to say the Ghost name, just ask the question, the best way is ask questions fast so you get an answer fast too, I like asking "Where are you?", "Are you here?" and "What do you want?".

There is a good trick, just let the Spirit Box on and you don't even need to ask any question! If the ghost has Spirit Box it will eventually say something. I personally don't like doing this too much because the Spirit Box sound is annoying and it gets in the way of other sounds, like doors closing or objects being thrown, but it is something useful anyhow.

Ghost Writing Book

There is no trick here, just put the book inside the ghost room, sometimes it takes forever and sometimes the Ghost writes it pretty fast.

I lied, there is one small trick to know if there isn't Ghost Writing, if you see the ghost throwing the book you can be sure there won't be any Ghost Writing. I like to put the Ghost Book right below or on top of D.O.T.S so I know if the book moved a little.

EMF Reader

If you hear something being thrown run there and put that EMF Reader to work EMF is really useful to take photos too, if the EMF is beeping you can still take a picure of what was interacted with.

  1. EMF 2: Ghost interacted with something (like opening doors).
  2. EMF 3: Ghost threw something.
  3. EMF 4: Manifestation / Ghost Event.
  4. EMF 5: Evidence (it has 25% chance of ocurring).

UV Flashlight

Nothing new to say here really, if you hear a door opening of closing run there with your UV Flashlight (or Glowstick) and check for fingerprints. Also check light switches if the Ghost turned any light on or off, check windows if you hear the Ghost hitting a window and check for footprints if the Ghost step on salt.

Flashlight

This Flashlight is weaker than the stronger flashlight and has a yellow light (while the strong one is white). I personally only play with the weak one, for me it makes the game a little more scarier and hard and I enjoy it, but that's a very personal choice.

Video Camera

You can use the Video Camera by holding it and walking around the house looking at the video screen, you might see some Ghost Orbs.

Placing the Video Camera in a good place that you can see most of the room will make the game a lot easier.

Photo Camera

Really useful to make some fat money, my advice is, always take pictures of door interaction if you do get fingerprints take 2 pictures, the next one will count as interaction.

Some people don't know that different photos means different money points, you need at least 500 points to get maximum money ($40), 400 to 499 points gives you $35, 300 to 399 $30 and goes on. Also, remember to take pictures close to what you want because that means how many stars you will get and how many points, here is the list of what gives more money points (I will only show 3 star points):

  1. Ghost: 100 points
  2. Bone: 70
  3. Cursed possession: 60
  4. Fingerprints: 50
  5. Footprints and interaction: 40
  6. Dirty water: 35
  7. Dead body: 30

That means if you want to make maximum money photo ($40) you need to take at least take photo of: Ghost, Bone, Cursed possession, and 7 footprints and / or interaction (all 3 stars). There are other combos that might make you reach 500 points.

D.O.T.S Projector

The best way to use DOTS is by putting it in a place that covers most of the Ghost Room, if you have 2 DOTS put one a little far away from the other so you can cover more of the room. I also don't recomment put it too close to a Video Camera because the camera will be too bright to see something.

Secondary Equipment

Candle

Candles slow sanity drain in the darkness and also provides light source without attracting the Ghost. It is also useful to know if the Ghost is around.

The Ghost will ocasionally blow out a candle that is in 4 meters range of him and the candle will extinguish if not blown after 2.5 to 5 minutes.

Candles are essential to test for Onryo, check out the test HERE

Cursed Possession

Bone

Bone is a Cursed Item that will appear in EVERY GAME. It can spawn anywhere on the map, even below carpets, on top of sofas and tables, so look carefully for it. Always take a picture before picking it up. It can have many different forms.

Haunted Mirror

Just have a quick look at it, if you know the map it will more than enough, if you see nothing compromising then maybe wait a little and look again, since waiting will make the ghost look elsewhere on the room.

It will drain the player using the mirror sanity by 10% very quick, and will break if it reaches 0%.

Music Box

When activated walk around the map to find the Ghost, you will hear the singing louder the closer you are to the Ghost. If you get to at least 5 meters of the Ghost it will manifest and walk towards the Music Box.

If the Ghost touches the player with the Music Box or walk more than 5 seconds or the Music Box get thrown it will start a cursed hunt. If the Music Box is placed on the ground it won't hunt.

If the player using the Music Box reaches 0% sanity it will initiate a normal hunt.

Listening to the entire song will drain 75% sanity of any player that hears it.

Ouija Board

Ask a question, get an answer, the best question to ask is "Where are you?" so you know what room exactly the ghost is, but it will drain 40% sanity of the player that asked the question, if you already know where the ghost is, don't ask this. You can also ask "Do you respond to everyone?" to know if the Ghost responds the Spirit Box only if you are alone or not (it will drain 20% sanity).

Ouija is good to trick a EMF 5 if the Ghost has that evidence you can ask questions that drain 5% sanity (the lowest), for example "How are you?" try asking 2 to 3 times to check if it will have an EMF 5 (there's a 25% chance of happening if that's an evidence).

Another useful use is taking picture of every question, it will count as interaction, so ask 5% sanity question and take as many pictures as you want (1 pic per question).

Summoning Circle

No tips here, light all 5 candles, take a picture, run.

Each candle decreases the player that lights it sanity by 16%, a total of 80% if it lights all candles.

Tarot Cards

There are 10 cards that can be pulled and 10 different effects that can happen.

  1. THE SUN: Restores sanity to 100%
  2. THE MOON: Drops sanity to 0%
  3. THE TOWER: Trigger an interaction
  4. THE WHEEL OF FORTUNE: Gains or loses 25% sanity (if it burns green or red)
  5. THE DEVIL: Trigger a Ghost Event towards the nearest player to the Ghost
  6. THE HIGH PRIESTESS: Revives randomly a dead player (if no one is dead it will revive the first that dies)
  7. THE HANGED MAN: Kills the player
  8. DEATH: Trigger a Cursed Hunt
  9. THE HERMIT: Traps the Ghost to its room for 1 minute
  10. THE FOOL: Will appear as other card then will show THE FOOL and nothing will happen

Voodoo Doll

The Voodoo Doll is really useful to get evidence, probably the best Cursed Object for it. So wait until you found the Ghost Room and use it to trigger evidence.

It can be used up to 10 times, using it drops 5% sanity and will pin randomly a part of the body and will trigger an interaction, any interaction even EMF 5, D.O.T.S, Ghost Writing and Fingerprints.

If the pin hits the Doll's heart it will drop 10% sanity and a Cursed Hunt will start.
